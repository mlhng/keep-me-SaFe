Our Motive

Why is walking so dangerous?

In 2020, more than 6,700 pedestrians were killed while walking and using wheelchairs, despite a dramatic decrease in the number of cars on the road and the number of miles traveled. Traffic collisions and deaths are preventable and often occur as a result of the way transportation in the United States has been designed – for the fast, uninterrupted movement of vehicles.
